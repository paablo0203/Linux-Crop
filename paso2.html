2. CROP CLASSIFICATION SYSTEM

OBJECTIVE:

Separate crops according to their quality, size, and ripeness to ensure marketing standards.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

- Artificial vision cameras analyze the shape, color, and defects of the product.

- Artificial intelligence algorithms classify and group products according to predefined categories.

- Automated conveyor belt systems direct products to their corresponding destination.

BENEFITS:

- Fast and efficient classification.

- Increased quality of the final product.

- Reduction of human errors and losses.
