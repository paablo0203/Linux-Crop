3. FOOD PREPARATION SYSTEM

OBJECTIVE:

Process crops for consumption or transformation into other products.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

- Automated washing and disinfection with hygiene sensors.

- Peeling, cutting, and mechanical processing according to the type of product.

- Incorporation of additives or cold preservation if necessary.

BENEFITS:

- Greater hygiene and food safety.

- Faster and more efficient processing.

- Reduction of losses due to deterioration.
