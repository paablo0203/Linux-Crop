5. PACKAGING SYSTEM

OBJECTIVE:

Ensure efficient packaging for the distribution and commercialization of crops.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

- Robotic systems automate packaging with precision and speed.

- Weight and volume sensors adjust the amount of product per container.

- Sustainable packaging materials to reduce environmental impact.

BENEFITS:

- Faster and more uniform packaging.

- Less waste of materials and products.

- Facilitates distribution and final sale.
