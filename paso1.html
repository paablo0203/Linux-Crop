1. CROP COLLECTION SYSTEM

OBJECTIVE:

Automate crop harvesting, reducing time and manual effort.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

- Optical and ripeness sensors detect the optimal harvest time.

- Robotic arms or mechanized harvesters collect the crops with precision.

- Geolocation systems optimize the collection route to minimize waste.

BENEFITS

- Reduction of labor costs.

- Greater efficiency and precision in harvesting.

- Less waste and damage to crops.
