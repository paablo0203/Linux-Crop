4. STORAGE SYSTEM

OBJECTIVE:

Preserve crops in optimal conditions until distribution or sale.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

- Use of temperature and humidity sensors to control storage conditions.

- Automated refrigeration systems for perishable products.

- Real-time monitoring to prevent losses and optimize storage space.

BENEFITS:

- Longer shelf life of the product.

- Reduction of waste due to poor storage.

- Optimization of logistical costs.
